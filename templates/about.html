OUR STORY

Bridging Technology & Agriculture

AgroMate was born out of a simple question: why can't farmers have access to the same data-driven insights that large agribusinesses do?

We built a suite of machine learning tools — accessible from any smartphone — that put crop prediction, fertilizer advisory, plant disease detection, and weather-based farming insights directly into the farmer's hands.

Today AgroMate serves over 10,000 farmers across Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh, and we're expanding every month.
